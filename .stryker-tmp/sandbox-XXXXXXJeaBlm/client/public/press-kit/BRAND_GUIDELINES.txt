ABOUT TALES OF ANERIA
================================================

Tales of Aneria is an epic tabletop RPG live play series that brings together passionate storytellers and adventurers to explore fantastical worlds through the lens of Dungeons & Dragons. Our show features multiple interconnected campaigns spanning diverse realms including Aneria, Pterrordale, and Journeys Through Taebrin.

TAGLINE:
"An epic journey through a world of magic, mystery, and adventure"

DESCRIPTION (Short):
An epic TTRPG live play series exploring the mystical realm of Aneria. Join us on our adventures every week.

DESCRIPTION (Long):
Tales of Aneria is a tabletop RPG actual play series that chronicles the adventures of heroes across multiple campaign settings. Combining rich storytelling, compelling characters, and authentic D&D gameplay, we bring epic fantasy adventures to life through engaging video content and podcast episodes. Our community of TTRPG enthusiasts enjoys weekly episodes, behind-the-scenes content, and deep dives into our custom worlds.

================================================

- Name: "Tales of Aneria" (always capitalize T, o, and A)

Primary Purple: #9333EA (RGB: 147, 51, 234)

TONE & VOICE:
